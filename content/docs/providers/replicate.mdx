## Prerequisites

- LocalProxy installed and running
- A Replicate account (create one at [replicate.com](https://replicate.com))

## Step 1: Create a Replicate API Token

1. **Log in to Replicate**

   - Navigate to [replicate.com/account/api-tokens](https://replicate.com/account/api-tokens)

2. **Create a New Token**
   - Click **Create token** and name it (e.g., “LocalProxy Integration”)

**Important**: Copy the token immediately after creation.

## Step 2: Configure Replicate in LocalProxy

1. **Open LocalProxy Settings**

   - Launch LocalProxy and click **Settings** in the sidebar

2. **Navigate to AI Providers**

   - Select **AI Providers**, then click **Add**

3. **Add Replicate Provider**

   - From the provider list, select **Replicate**

4. **Enter Connection Details**

   - **Base URL**: Leave default unless using a proxy (default `https://api.replicate.com/v1`)
   - **API Key**: Paste your Replicate token
   - **Model preferences**: Provide the model slug or owner/name (e.g., `meta/meta-llama-3-8b-instruct:latest`, `mistralai/mixtral-8x7b-instruct:latest`)

5. **Save Configuration**
   - Click **Save** and ensure Replicate appears in your AI Providers
